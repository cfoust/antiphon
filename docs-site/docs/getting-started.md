# Getting started

You've [installed the app](./install.md) and connected an agent. Here's how Antiphon behaves
day to day.

## The room

The main window is a top-down radar: you're the pupil at the center, agents are dots seated
around you. Drag an agent to move it — its voice follows, and you'll hear a soft sonar pulse
from the new position. Seats persist across restarts.

The list on the right shows every session: status, last message, and a waiting indicator.
Hover a row to spotlight the agent in the room. Snooze one (the moon) to move it out of the
world — it keeps receiving updates and rejoins where it left off when you wake it.

The eye button in the corner is the master switch. Closed eye: camera released, output
silent, window dimmed. Open: watching and listening.

## What you'll hear

Each agent has a musical identity — a chord built on its ping note:

- **Tool ticks.** Each tool call plucks the next note of the agent's descending chord.
  You learn who's busy without looking.
- **The working hum.** An agent deep in a long task settles into a low three-tone machine
  hum at the bottom of its register.
- **The waiting bloom.** An agent that finished and wants you builds a slow minor-seventh
  bloom in one ear — over minutes, not milliseconds. It never demands; it accumulates.
- **Speech.** When an agent completes something, it says so — a sentence or two, spoken in
  its own voice, from its seat.

## Close your eyes

The webcam tracks your head pose and eye state (all on-device — nothing leaves your
machine).

With eyes open, speech is held back; you get the ambient layer only. **Close your eyes and
the room comes up**: a short fade, and every agent is exactly where its dot is. Turn your
head toward one and hold it there a beat — a quiet crest marks the lock, and if it has news,
it delivers its summary. Look to the next voice; repeat. It's triage, by ear, in seconds.

Open your eyes and the world recedes. If an agent spoke, a reply box is waiting: type (or
dictate) and press enter, and your words are injected into that agent's session.

## Settings

The gear opens settings in-window:

- **Fit** — a frequency-scale slider matching the HRTF to your ears; a looped voice plays
  straight ahead while you adjust. Set it once where the voice sounds most *outside your
  head*.
- **Tracking** — camera choice and recalibration.
- **Voices** — TTS providers, API keys, and per-voice toggles.
- **Language** — English, Русский, 简体中文, 繁體中文.
